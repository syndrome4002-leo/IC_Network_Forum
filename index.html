Welcome to the ICN Legacy Support Forum. Begun in the 1990's as the very first support IC support group on the internet, we are very proud of our work in the past 30 years. Sixty thousand members, hundreds of thousands of posts and a vibrant community of patients from over 90 countries. However, times have changed and so must we. Patients now prefer watching videos (http://www.youtube.com/icnjill) and our live IC support group meetings held every Sunday at 1PM PST, 4 PM EST. Learn more about our support services at: https://www.icnetwork.org/ic-support-center/

Please note that this forum is no longer interactive. All user accounts have been deleted and the original forum software is now gone. We have, however, retained the data for research purposes. This is a very large forum, and pages may load slowly. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to reach out to the ICN office at: icnetwork.org. We welcome phone calls as well at: 800-928-7496 or 707-538-9442.
