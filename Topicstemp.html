      Because we have members of all political groups and from countries all over the world, we have in the past, and will continue to ask members to avoid posting political messages, with the only exception being to encourage members to vote.

      This includes discussions regarding health care...


      Every September, the IC community rallies together during IC Awareness Month to educate, empower and support the many patients who have suffered in silence at home alone. This year, we hope to reach patients who are struggling with treatment and/or not responding to typical bladder therapies....
      We expect our users to conduct themselves as they would in any support group..with courtesy, kindness, and respect for each other. We do not appreciate posts that promote conflict, that include personal attacks, or that are resentful of others postings. We expect our users to debate the message not...
      Half of the IC patient population is ecstatic right now while the other half is stressed and worried about their health care. This is a delicate dance for anyone to walk. I want you to know that the ICN doesn't ask what party you are from if you call for help. We are here for anyone who is struggling....
